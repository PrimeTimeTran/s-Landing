Customer Engagement Platform

Let's face the facts. We know that you want to develop a deeper understanding of your customers. Believe it or not, they want to get to know you too. The problem is, when they're visiting, you're working, and when you're free, they're gone.

On Stage, the chat centers around your business because we all know that's where the fun is. Even after the lights got off you can still engage with customers in order to connect with them.

  • Venue based chat so that you can communicate later
  • Content curation so you can show your true colors
  • Data analytics for deeper understandings
  • Premium features for all the extras you want
